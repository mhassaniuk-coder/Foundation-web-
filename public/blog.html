Celebrating Our 500th Job Placement!

This month we reached a major milestone in our employment services program.

Posted March 15, 2024 | 5 min read

This week, we celebrated our 500th successful job placement! Marcus, a man who was homeless just 18 months ago, landed his dream job as a warehouse supervisor. But Marcus's story is just one of many.

Our job training and employment services program has transformed lives across our community. From resume workshops to interview coaching to on-the-job support, we've helped nearly 500 men find meaningful work and build sustainable futures.
