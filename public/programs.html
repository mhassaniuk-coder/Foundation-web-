Programs & Services

Comprehensive support for men and boys in need.

🏘️ Street Outreach

We meet men and boys where they are—on the streets, in shelters, and in the most vulnerable situations. Our outreach teams provide:

  • ✓ Medical care and health assessments
  • ✓ Hot meals and emergency supplies
  • ✓ Hygiene kits and clothing
  • ✓ Connection to housing resources
  • ✓ Mental health and addiction support referrals
  • ✓ Case management and advocacy
  • ✓ Social security and benefits assistance

Building relationships and trust, one interaction at a time.

🏘️

Real Impact in Real Time

Our outreach teams make hundreds of connections each month, providing immediate help and pathways to long-term support.

👨‍🏫

One-on-One Change

Mentorship is the heart of transformation. Our mentors walk alongside individuals through their journey.

👨‍🏫 Mentorship Programs

Every man and boy deserves someone who believes in them. Our mentors provide:

  • ✓ Life coaching and goal setting
  • ✓ Job interview preparation
  • ✓ Financial literacy training
  • ✓ Emotional support and accountability
  • ✓ Connection to community and networks
  • ✓ Conflict resolution skills
  • ✓ Personal development guidance

Duration: 6-12 months with ongoing support options.

💼 Job Training & Employment

Sustainable independence starts with employment. We provide:

  • ✓ Skills training programs
  • ✓ Resume and cover letter coaching
  • ✓ Interview preparation (mock interviews)
  • ✓ Job placement assistance
  • ✓ Apprenticeship opportunities
  • ✓ Work readiness training
  • ✓ On-the-job support and mentoring
  • ✓ Long-term career development

89% Job Placement Rate

💼

Career Pathways

From job readiness to promotion advancement, we support sustainable employment and career growth.
