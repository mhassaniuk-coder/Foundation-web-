Program 01

🌃 Street Outreach

Meeting men and boys where they are—on the streets, in shelters, and in the most vulnerable situations.

Our outreach teams build relationships through consistent, compassionate presence. We don't wait for people to come to us; we go to them, offering immediate help and pathways to long-term support.

What We Provide:

✓ Medical care & assessments
✓ Hot meals & supplies
✓ Hygiene kits & clothing
✓ Housing resources
✓ Mental health referrals
✓ Case management

Program 02

👥 Mentorship Programs

Every man and boy deserves someone who believes in them and will walk alongside them.

Our mentorship program pairs individuals with trained mentors who provide guidance, accountability, and genuine care. This isn't a one-time meeting—it's a committed relationship that lasts 6-12 months or longer.

Mentorship Includes:

✓ Life coaching & goal setting
✓ Job interview preparation
✓ Financial literacy training
✓ Emotional support
✓ Community connections
✓ Conflict resolution skills

Program 03

🎓 Educational Workshops

Knowledge is power. Our educational programs equip men and boys with the skills and confidence they need to succeed.

💰

Financial Literacy

Budgeting, saving, credit building, and financial planning. Learn to manage money and build wealth for the future.

🧠

Mental Health & Wellness

Understanding mental health, coping strategies, stress management, and building emotional resilience.

💬

Communication Skills

Effective communication, conflict resolution, relationship building, and professional interaction skills.

📱

Digital Literacy

Computer basics, internet safety, online job searching, and essential technology skills for today's world.

⚖️

Legal Rights & Resources

Understanding your rights, navigating legal systems, expungement assistance, and accessing legal aid.

❤️

Health & Nutrition

Physical wellness, nutrition education, exercise programs, and accessing healthcare resources.

Program 04

💼 Job Training & Employment

Sustainable independence starts with meaningful employment. We provide comprehensive job readiness support.

From resume writing to interview preparation, from skills training to job placement, we walk alongside individuals through every step of their employment journey.

Our Employment Services:

  • 📋
    Resume & Cover Letter Coaching

    Professional documents that get noticed by employers.

  • 🎤
    Interview Preparation

    Mock interviews, confidence building, and professional presentation.

  • 🔧
    Skills Training Programs

    Trade skills, certifications, and vocational training opportunities.

  • 🤝
    Employer Partnerships

    Direct connections to employers who believe in second chances.
