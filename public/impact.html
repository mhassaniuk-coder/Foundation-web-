Success Stories

These are real stories from real men transformed by our programs. Names changed to protect privacy.

👤

Marcus

"From Homeless to Employed"

A Second Chance

The Challenge: Marcus spent 3 years homeless after losing his job due to untreated PTSD. He had lost touch with his family and hope for the future.

Our Support: Street outreach teams connected Marcus with housing resources. Through our mentorship program, he received one-on-one support, mental health referrals, and job training.

The Result: Today, Marcus has been employed for 18 months at a local construction company where he's been promoted to supervisor. He's reconnected with his daughter and volunteers with our outreach team.

"This foundation gave me my life back. I'm not defined by my worst moment."

👨‍🦳

James

"Recovery & Redemption"

Breaking the Cycle

The Challenge: James struggled with addiction for two decades, lost custody of his children, and had been in and out of the criminal justice system.

Our Support: Our mentors connected him with recovery programs. Through support groups, job training, and consistent encouragement, James rebuilt his life with structure.

The Result: James is 3 years sober, works at a local nonprofit, and has regained joint custody of his youngest son. He's studying to become a peer counselor.

"I never thought I'd be a father again. Restored Kings didn't just save my life—they restored it."

👦

David

"A Young Man's Path"

Education & Opportunity

The Challenge: David aged out of foster care at 18 with no family support, living in shelters, and facing an uncertain future.

Our Support: Our mentors helped him access education benefits, apply to community college, and develop job skills through our training programs.

The Result: David is now pursuing an associate's degree in IT, working part-time, and has moved into transitional housing. He dreams of becoming a software developer.

"My mentor believed in me when I didn't believe in myself. That changed everything."
