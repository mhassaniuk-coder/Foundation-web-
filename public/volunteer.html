Volunteer Opportunities

👨‍🏫

Mentor

Commitment: 2-4 hours per week for 6-12 months

Be a trusted guide for someone rebuilding their life. Mentors help with goal-setting, job prep, life skills, and emotional support.

Requirements: Compassion, reliability, ability to listen. Training provided.

🏘️

Outreach Worker

Commitment: 4-8 hours per week (weekends available)

Help our teams connect with homeless and at-risk men. Distribute supplies, connect people to resources, build relationships.

Requirements: Physical ability to be outdoors, comfort in difficult situations. Full training provided.

🎉

Event Volunteer

Commitment: 4-8 hours per event (flexible schedule)

Help organize and run community events, workshops, and celebrations. Registration, setup, hospitality.

Requirements: Enthusiasm and teamwork. Great for flexible availability.

💼

Skills-Based Volunteer

Commitment: Flexible (project-based)

Use your professional skills: Resume writing, interview coaching, legal help, counseling, accounting, marketing, web design, etc.

Requirements: Professional experience in your field. Can work virtually.
