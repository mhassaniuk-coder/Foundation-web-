Our Story

Why We Exist

Too many men and boys are left behind. Homeless on the streets. Struggling with addiction. Exploited for labor or resources. Forgotten by society.

The Restored Kings Foundation exists to restore their dignity and help them rebuild their lives with strength and purpose.

We believe every man and boy deserves:

  • ✓ A safe place to sleep and recover
  • ✓ Access to education and job training
  • ✓ Mentorship from men who care
  • ✓ A community that believes in them
